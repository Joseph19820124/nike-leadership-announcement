✎ Biography | 个人简介
English

Cathy Sparks is a 25+ year Nike veteran who has built her career from the ground up, starting as a store athlete at Niketown Portland in 1996. Her journey from the retail floor to the executive suite exemplifies Nike's culture of developing talent from within.

Throughout her tenure, Sparks has led teams across all four of Nike's geographies, developing deep expertise in marketplace transformation and consumer-led growth strategies. Her unique experience spans retail operations, direct-to-consumer business, digital commerce, and regional general management.

Known for building strong teams and moving with urgency, Sparks has consistently demonstrated the ability to bring Nike's magic back to markets that need it most. Her leadership style combines operational excellence with a genuine passion for athletes and consumers.

"Cathy has lived and worked across multiple regions, including Asia, and brings deep intuition for athletes*, consumers, and the role Nike plays at the intersection of sport and culture. She builds strong teams, moves with urgency, and knows how to bring Nike's magic back to markets that need it most."
— Elliott Hill, President & CEO, NIKE, Inc.

Sparks is excited to return to Asia with her family—a region she knows well and loves—and to lead Nike's next chapter in Greater China, one of the company's most critical markets.

Education

Cathy Sparks attended Elon University. She is based in the Portland, Oregon Metropolitan Area.

中文

Cathy Sparks是一位拥有25年以上Nike经验的资深领导者，她的职业生涯从1996年在Niketown Portland担任门店员工开始，一步步成长起来。她从零售一线走向高管层的历程，充分体现了Nike内部培养人才的企业文化。

在她的任期内，Sparks曾领导Nike所有四个地区的团队，在市场转型和以消费者为导向的增长战略方面积累了深厚的专业知识。她独特的经验涵盖零售运营、直接面向消费者业务、数字商务和区域综合管理。

Sparks以建立强大团队和快速行动著称，她始终展现出将Nike的魔力带回最需要它的市场的能力。她的领导风格将卓越运营与对运动员和消费者的真挚热情相结合。

"Cathy曾在多个地区生活和工作，包括亚洲，她对运动员*、消费者以及Nike在体育与文化交汇处所扮演的角色有着深刻的直觉。她建立强大的团队，行动迅速，知道如何将Nike的魔力带回最需要它的市场。"
— Elliott Hill，NIKE, Inc. 总裁兼首席执行官

Sparks很高兴能与家人一起回到亚洲——一个她熟悉并热爱的地区——并领导Nike在大中华区的下一个篇章，这是公司最关键的市场之一。

教育背景

Cathy Sparks毕业于伊隆大学(Elon University)。她目前居住在美国俄勒冈州波特兰都会区。
