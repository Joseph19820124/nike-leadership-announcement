✎ New Role | 新职位
English

Erin Moscow will join the Nike Brand Leadership Team as VP, Global Merchandising, reporting to Amy Montagne, President of Nike. In this role, Erin will oversee Merchandising as well as Business Integration and Sports Analytics.

Key Responsibilities

  • Global Merchandising Strategy Set the direction for Nike's global merchandising strategy
  • Cross-Functional Collaboration Work closely with product, global, and geo merchandising teams to shape the line plan
  • Assortment Frameworks Deliver consistent, marketplace-right assortment frameworks that serve athletes* globally
  • Operational Excellence Accelerate operational excellence and cross-functional integration

This appointment follows César Garcia's move to VP/GM, EMEA, creating this opportunity within the Nike Brand Leadership Team.

👤 Biography | 个人简介
English

With more than 20 years of merchandising experience, Erin Moscow has built an impressive global career across the apparel and sport industry, always with a relentless focus on the consumer.

Since joining Nike in 2014, she has held leadership roles across Global, North America, EMEA, and APLA — shaping product strategies that fuel growth, strengthen brand distinction, and meet the evolving needs of athletes and consumers worldwide.

Beyond her passion for product, storytelling, and brand connection, Erin is a people-first leader who is committed to developing the next generation of merchandising talent.

Global Experience

Global Global Merchandising Leadership
North America Regional Merchandising Leadership
EMEA Europe, Middle East & Africa

Personal Background

Originally from Canada

Erin, her husband Elliot, and their daughters, Madison and Maisie, have lived around the world and maintain a deep connection to sport.

Family Sports Passions:

Skiing Fencing Soccer Lacrosse
